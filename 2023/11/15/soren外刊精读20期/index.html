Unit2

🚩sparkle

​ sparkle表示“出色”时，也可以跟 shine, dazzle, excel, impress, stand out, gleam 等词替换。

🚩allure 吸引力、诱惑力、魅力＝attraction, charm, appeal, magnetism, fascination.

​ ❗the allure draws sb /have a strong allure for sb“某物吸引某人”

​ eg.The allure of Paris draws millions of tourists each year.巴黎的魅力每年吸引着数百万游客。

​ eg.The offer of a well-paying job had a strong allure for him.一份薪水丰厚的工作提供对他有很强的吸引力。

​ ❗the allure lies in ,the allure rests on ”某物的魅力在于“

​ eg.The allure of a mystery novel lies in its suspense and intrigue.悬疑小说的魅力在于它的悬念和情节。

​ ❗strong/overwhelming“吸引力大”

​ ❗add to sb’s/sth’s allure“某个品质给人增添了魅力”

​ Her confidence added to her overall allure.她自信增添了她的整体魅力。

🚩the appeal of…rests on..“…的吸引力在于…”

eg.As is often the case with Apple, much of the iPod’s appeal rests on its design and its user interface, which seems much simpler and more fluid than the competitors’. 正如惯常所见的苹果风格，iPod的吸引力很大程度上取决于其设计和用户界面，这些似乎比竞品更为简单流畅。（或：苹果之道常是如此。iPod吸引力，多仗其精致的设计和流畅的用户界面，胜过诸多竞争者。）

eg.The appeal of Game of Thrones rests in its progressive take on a traditional, fictional setting inspired by medieval Western civilization. 《权力的游戏》之魅力，在于它对受中世纪西方文明启发的传统虚构背景进行了前卫的演绎。

🚩for表目的，可理解为用于

eg.a machine for slicing bread 切面包片机

eg.Are you learning English for pleasure or for your work? 你学英语是出于消遣还是为了工作？

for a ring on finger 修饰前面的diamond，意思是，用于指尖戒指上的钻石。
